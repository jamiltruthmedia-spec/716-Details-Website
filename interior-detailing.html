Common Interior Issues We Solve

Stubborn Stains & Spills

Coffee, food, ink, and other stains that regular cleaning can't remove. Our professional extraction equipment and specialized products eliminate even the most persistent stains from carpets and upholstery.

Persistent Odors

Smoke, pet odors, food smells, and musty odors that linger despite cleaning attempts. Our ozone treatment and deep cleaning process eliminates odors at the molecular level.

Cracked & Faded Leather

Leather seats and trim that have become dry, cracked, or faded from sun exposure and age. Our conditioning treatments restore suppleness and protect against further damage.

Pet Hair & Allergens

Embedded pet hair and allergens that regular vacuuming can't remove. Our specialized tools and techniques extract hair from fabric fibers and eliminate allergens.

Worn High-Touch Areas

Steering wheels, gear shifters, and door handles that show wear and grime buildup. We restore these areas with appropriate cleaning and protection products.

Neglected Luxury Interiors

High-end vehicles with specialized materials that need expert care. We have experience with exotic leathers, Alcantara, carbon fiber, and other luxury interior materials.

Interior Detailing FAQ

How often should I get my car's interior detailed?

We recommend professional interior detailing every 3-6 months for regular use vehicles, or monthly for high-use vehicles with pets or children. This maintains cleanliness, protects materials, and preserves resale value.

Can you remove pet odors and stains completely?

Yes, we specialize in pet odor and stain removal using professional extraction equipment and ozone treatment. Most pet-related issues can be completely eliminated with our advanced techniques.

Is it safe to clean leather seats with water-based products?

We use only pH-balanced, leather-safe products specifically designed for automotive leather. Our conditioning treatments actually improve leather suppleness and provide protection against cracking and fading.

Do you offer mobile interior detailing services?

Yes, we provide mobile interior detailing throughout Niagara County. Our mobile units are equipped with extraction equipment, steam cleaners, and all necessary products for complete interior detailing at your location.
