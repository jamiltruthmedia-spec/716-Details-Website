New York Tint Laws & Compliance

Front Side Windows

Must allow more than 70% of light in (70% VLT minimum)

Back Side Windows

Any darkness can be used (no VLT restriction)

Rear Window

Any darkness can be used (no VLT restriction)

Windshield

Non-reflective tint allowed above AS-1 line

We ensure all installations comply with New York State regulations. Our team stays updated on current laws to keep you legal and protected.
